Cispy pork kra phao recipe

Description

Thai crispy pork kra phao, also known as Pad Kra Pao Moo, is a popular Thai dish characterized by its spicy and flavorful stir-fry made with ground pork, Thai holy basil, garlic, and a savory sauce.

Ingredients

  • Ground pork: The main protein used in the dish.
  • Holy basil: A key ingredient that adds a bold flavor.
  • Garlic: Adds depth and aroma to the stir-fry.
  • Thai chilies: For added heat and flavor.
  • Oyster sauce, fish sauce, and soy sauce: These create a savory sauce for the pork.
  • Long beans (or snake beans): Adds texture and crunch to the dish.
  • Oil: A neutral oil is used for stir-frying.

Steps

  1. Prepare the Ingredients: Gather ground pork, Thai holy basil, garlic, Thai chili peppers, vegetable oil, and a savory sauce made from oyster sauce, fish sauce, and a little sugar. You can also add dark soy sauce for a sweeter flavor, but it's not necessary.
  2. Cook the Pork: Heat vegetable oil in a wok or skillet over high heat. Add minced pork and cook until browned.
  3. Add Aromatics: Add garlic and chili peppers to the wok and stir-fry until fragrant. 5
  4. Combine Ingredients: Add the holy basil and the savory sauce, stirring to combine. Cook for a few minutes until the pork is tender and the sauce is well absorbed. 5
  5. Serve: Serve the dish over steamed jasmine rice and top with a crispy fried egg.
