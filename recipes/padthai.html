Pad Thai Recipe

Description

This delicious Pad Thai recipe combines rice noodles, protein, and a flavorful sauce, making it a quick and satisfying meal that you can prepare in about 30 minutes.

  • Noodles: 8 oz thin rice noodles
  • Protein: 1 cup firm tofu, chicken, or shrimp (your choice)
  • Vegetables: 1 cup bean sprouts, 1/2 cup chopped green onions, and 1/2 cup red bell pepper (optional)
  • Eggs: 2 large eggs
  • Peanuts: 1/4 cup crushed peanuts for garnish
  • 3 tablespoons fish sauce
  • 2 tablespoons tamarind paste
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon Sriracha
  • 1 tablespoon peanut butter
