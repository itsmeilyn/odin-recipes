Pad Thai Recipe

Description

This delicious Pad Thai recipe combines rice noodles, protein, and a flavorful sauce, making it a quick and satisfying meal that you can prepare in about 30 minutes.

  • Noodles: 8 oz thin rice noodles
  • Protein: 1 cup firm tofu, chicken, or shrimp (your choice)
  • Vegetables: 1 cup bean sprouts, 1/2 cup chopped green onions, and 1/2 cup red bell pepper (optional)
  • Eggs: 2 large eggs
  • Peanuts: 1/4 cup crushed peanuts for garnish
  • 3 tablespoons fish sauce
  • 2 tablespoons tamarind paste
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon Sriracha
  • 1 tablespoon peanut butter

Steps

  1. Prepare the Noodles: Soak the rice noodles in hot water for about 20-30 minutes until they are tender but not mushy. Drain and set aside.
  2. Make the Sauce: In a bowl, mix together the fish sauce, tamarind paste, brown sugar, Sriracha, and peanut butter until well combined. Set aside.
  3. Cook the Protein: In a large pan or wok, heat 1-2 tablespoons of oil over medium-high heat. Add your chosen protein (tofu, chicken, or shrimp) and cook until browned and cooked through. Remove from the pan and set aside.
  4. Scramble the Eggs: In the same pan, crack the eggs and scramble them until just set. Push them to one side of the pan.
  5. Combine Ingredients: Add the soaked noodles to the pan along with the sauce. Toss everything together, adding the cooked protein, bean sprouts, and green onions. Stir-fry for a few minutes until everything is heated through and well combined.
  6. Serve: Plate the Pad Thai and garnish with crushed peanuts and additional bean sprouts if desired. Serve with lime wedges on the side for an extra burst of flavor.
